## 1. Introduction

Main points:

- Dendrite and spine segmentation in two-photon microscopy is difficult.
- Ground truth annotation is expensive and error-prone.
- EM-derived 3D meshes provide detailed structural information.
- This project investigates whether such meshes can synthesize useful two-photon-like data.
- The work compares rendering methods, noise models, computational cost, and DeepD3 performance.
